Dr. Beverly Eramo - Psychologist

AHPRA Registered and endorsed Clinical Psychologist • Foundation Member of the Australian Clinical Psychologists’ Association • Member: Australian and New Zealand Association of Psychiatrists, Psychologists and the Law • Member: Australian and New Zealand Mental Health Association • Registered Provider for Medicare, TAC, Work Cover
